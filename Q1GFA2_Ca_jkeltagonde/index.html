Volleyball

Volleyball is a team sport, invented in 1895 by William G. Morgan in which two teams of six players are separated by a net. In volleyball, there are seven (7) positions namely the Outside Hitter, Right Side Hitter, Opposite Hitter, Setter, Middle Blocker, Libero, and Defensive Specialist. Each team tries to score points by grounding a ball on the other team's court under organized rules. The players use their hands to bat a ball back and forth over a high net, trying to make the ball touch the court within the opponents' playing area before it can be returned. A team is allowed only three touches of the ball before it must be returned over the net.

Learning volleyball is fun!
